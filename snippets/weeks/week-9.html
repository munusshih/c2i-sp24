Assignments

  • Artist Study

    Vera Molnar, Ellsworth Kelly, Jean Arp, and Hélio Oiticica all worked with basic shapes, color, and elements of chance (or so it seems). Your task is to generate a new work in the style of one of these artists using code.

    Pay particular attention to the shapes, color, subtleties, and multiplicity (e.g. the amount of elements) of your chosen artist’s work. Consider where you can incorporate randomness, and if you need to use a for loop to target multiple elements at once. Even better: if you want more control over the randomness, consider using conditional statements!

    As always, create a new subfolder called exercise-6 within your username.github.io folder. Create a new index.html, styles.css, and script.js file within that folder to work in.

    To get full credit, make sure you hit all the requirements in the checklist below.

    1. I submitted a URL to my Artist Study by 4/5 at 9 AM ET.

    2. This exercise is linked out from my class homepage.

    3. I emulated the style of one of the above artists and reinterpreted their work in a new way.

    4. I incorporated randomness where appropriate.
